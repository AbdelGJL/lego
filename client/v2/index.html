Makes a Great Deal?

We analyze each potential deal through three critical lenses:

1. Price Intelligence

We don't just look at the sticker price. Our algorithm compares the deal's price against historical sales data, using advanced statistical techniques. We're looking for significant savings - typically a price reduction between 20% and 30%, or an exceptional drop of 40% or more. This ensures you're not just buying something cheap, but getting genuine value.

2. Community and Platform Dynamics

Different platforms tell different stories about a deal's quality:

On Dealabs:
  • Fresh deals (less than a day old) need buzz: at least one comment and a temperature above 80°
  • Slightly older deals (1-2 days) require more engagement: over 3 comments and a temperature above 120°
  • Established deals (2+ days) need serious traction: more than 10 comments and a temperature over 160°
On Vinted:
  • We track the market's recent activity by looking at sales dates.
  • We check if similar items have been sold within the last month, indicating a healthy, active market

3. Resale Potential

We calculate your potential profit margin. A good deal isn't just cheap - it needs room for potential resale. We aim for deals with at least a 20% margin, giving you a meaningful financial upside. Our Recommendation System Based on how many of these criteria a deal meets, we provide clear guidance:

  • 0 criteria met: Avoid this deal
  • 1 criterion met: Proceed with caution
  • 2 criteria met: Worth considering
  • 3 criteria met: This is a must-grab opportunity!
